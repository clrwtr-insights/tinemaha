proc_dashboard_data/satellite_map/
----------------------------------------------
date: date of observation (Unit: year-month-day)
lat: date of observation (Unit: degree latitude)
lon: date of observation (Unit: degree longitude)
Chlorophyll: proportional to NIR/Red from Sentinel (Unit: nanometers -- nm)




proc_dashboard_data/weather_tab/
----------------------------------------------
date: date of observation (Unit: year-month-day)
lat: date of observation (Unit: degree latitude)
lon: date of observation (Unit: degree longitude)
Precip_mean: mean of average gprecipitation grids (Unit: millimeters -- mL)
Air_Temp_mean: mean of average air temperature grids (Unit: Celsius -- C)
Precip_cumsum: cumulative sum of average precipitation observed (Unit: millimeters -- mL)
Air_Temp_cumsum: cumulative sum of average air temperature (Unit: Celsius -- C)
